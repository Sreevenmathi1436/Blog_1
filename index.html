About Us

Welcome to Green Basket.
We are so excited that you’re here!

we bring you the best of nature's goodness — fresh, organic, and eco-friendly products crafted for your health and the planet. From herbal juices and essential oils to jute bags and wooden combs, everything we offer is chemical-free, cruelty-free, and sustainable.

Our journey began with a simple idea — to make it easy and affordable for everyone to choose natural alternatives over chemicals, plastic, and artificial additives.

“To reconnect people with nature by making organic and sustainable products a part of everyday life.”
Through Green Basket, we strive to build a community of conscious consumers who care not just about what they consume, but how it’s made, where it comes from, and who it helps..

Stay Natural. Stay Rooted. Go Green with Green Basket.
