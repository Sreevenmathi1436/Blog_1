Banana Chips

Date: 02.01.2023

Banana chips are a popular South Indian snack made from thinly sliced raw bananas, deep-fried in coconut oil, and seasoned with salt or spices.

Ingredients: Raw bananas, salt, turmeric, coconut oil.

Preparation:
  1. Peel and thinly slice raw bananas.
  2. Soak slices in salt water for a few minutes.
  3. Heat coconut oil and deep-fry the slices until golden brown.
  4. Drain excess oil and sprinkle with salt or spices.
  5. Store in an airtight container for freshness.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Date: 06.03.2024

A cheese sandwich is a simple yet delicious snack that can be made in minutes.

Ingredients: Bread, butter, cheese slices.

Preparation:
  1. Butter two slices of bread.
  2. Place a slice of cheese between them.
  3. Grill on a pan until golden brown and the cheese melts.
  4. Serve hot with ketchup or a side of salad.

Omelette: A Versatile Breakfast Favorite

Date: 15.07.2024

Omelettes are a protein-packed breakfast option that can be customized with various fillings like vegetables, cheese, or herbs.

Ingredients: Eggs, salt, pepper, optional veggies/cheese.

Preparation:
  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and pour the egg mixture.
  3. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, or cheese as desired.
  4. Cook until set, then fold and serve hot.
